Commercial Locksmith Services

Advanced security solutions for Grand Junction businesses

Protecting your business, employees, and assets requires professional-grade security solutions. Locksmith Grand Junction specializes in commercial locksmith services designed for offices, retail stores, warehouses, and industrial facilities throughout Grand Junction. From master key systems to high-security locks and panic bar installations, we provide comprehensive security solutions that meet your business needs and comply with local building codes.

Master Key Systems

Take control of your building's security with a professionally designed master key system. Allow managers access to all areas while restricting employees to specific zones. Perfect for offices, retail stores, apartment complexes, and educational facilities.

Master Key Benefits:

  • Hierarchical access control (master, sub-master, and individual keys)
  • Reduce key clutter for management staff
  • Easy to add or remove access for specific areas
  • Custom system design for your facility layout
  • Emergency override capabilities
  • Detailed documentation and key tracking

Smart Business Solution: A master key system simplifies building management while maintaining strict security protocols. Perfect for growing businesses that need flexible yet secure access control.

High-Security Lock Systems

Protect valuable inventory, sensitive documents, and expensive equipment with commercial-grade high-security locks. These advanced locking systems resist picking, drilling, and forced entry attempts that would defeat standard locks.

High-Security Features:

  • Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, and Assa Abloy systems
  • Pick-resistant and drill-resistant cylinders
  • Restricted keyway systems (keys can't be duplicated)
  • Patent-protected key control
  • UL 437 rated security
  • Perfect for safes, server rooms, and storage areas

Maximum Protection: High-security locks provide the ultimate defense against unauthorized entry. Ideal for businesses storing valuable assets, handling sensitive information, or requiring insurance compliance.

Panic Bars & Exit Devices

Ensure your business meets fire code requirements with properly installed panic bars and exit devices. We install, repair, and maintain all types of emergency exit hardware to keep your building code-compliant and your occupants safe.

Exit Device Services:

  • Panic bar installation and replacement
  • Fire-rated exit devices
  • Surface-mounted and concealed models
  • Emergency exit alarm integration
  • ADA-compliant installations
  • Annual inspection and maintenance

Code Compliance: Panic bars are required by law in many commercial buildings. Our installations meet all local fire codes and building regulations while ensuring smooth emergency egress.

Access Control Systems

Move beyond traditional keys with electronic access control. Issue key cards or fobs, track entry and exit times, and remotely grant or revoke access. Perfect for offices, gyms, medical facilities, and multi-tenant buildings.

Access Control Options:

  • Keycard and key fob systems
  • Keypad entry systems
  • Smartphone-based access
  • Biometric readers (fingerprint/facial recognition)
  • Integration with existing security systems
  • Cloud-based management platforms
  • Audit trails and access reports

Modern Security: Electronic access control provides accountability, convenience, and enhanced security. Instantly revoke access for former employees without rekeying locks or collecting keys.

Commercial Rekeying & Lock Changes

When employees leave, keys go missing, or you move into a new commercial space, rekeying ensures security. We rekey locks quickly and efficiently, minimizing disruption to your business operations.

Rekeying Services:

  • Complete building rekeying
  • Emergency after-hours rekeying
  • Lock replacement and upgrades
  • New key cutting and labeling
  • Same-day service for urgent needs
  • Minimal business disruption

Security Reset: Rekeying is more cost-effective than replacing all locks while providing the same security benefits. We work around your schedule to minimize business interruption.

Storefront Door Locks & Hardware

Storefront doors require specialized locking hardware that withstands heavy use while maintaining security. We install and service all types of commercial door locks and closers for glass storefronts and aluminum doors.

Storefront Services:

  • Mortise lock installation and repair
  • Door closer adjustment and replacement
  • Push/pull handle sets
  • Thumb-turn deadbolts
  • Floor closers and pivots
  • Glass door lock specialists

Heavy-Duty Solutions: Retail storefronts need durable hardware that handles constant use. Our commercial-grade products are built to last and maintain smooth operation.

File Cabinets, Desks & Safes

Secure sensitive documents and valuables with professional cabinet and safe services. We open locked file cabinets, install new locks, and service commercial safes of all types.

Cabinet & Safe Services:

  • File cabinet lock replacement and rekeying
  • Desk drawer lock installation
  • Safe opening and combination changes
  • Lock-out recovery (no damage methods)
  • Electronic safe programming
  • Master keying for multiple cabinets

Document Security: Protect confidential files, employee records, and important documents with reliable locking systems. We work with all major file cabinet brands.
